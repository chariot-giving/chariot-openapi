DAFpay Accounts is a DAF Provider–facing API. It lets you create accounts for your donors, register the funds they can give from, and process the grant requests they submit through DAFpay on nonprofit websites.


## What you get

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title='Invite-based onboarding' icon='paper-plane'>
    Create an account in your system, share the `auth_code` or `invite_url`, and the donor completes verification in DAFpay.
  </Card>
  <Card title='Multi-fund support' icon='layer-group'>
    Register one or many funds per account whenever you need to — often when an account is created or activated, but not only then. Update funds anytime as your offering changes.
  </Card>
  <Card title='Streamlined grant processing' icon='check-double'>
    Receive grant requests as donors submit them, and report `initiated` or `cancelled` back to DAFpay.
  </Card>
  <Card title='Webhooks' icon='bell'>
    Subscribe to account and grant request events so your system stays in sync with DAFpay in real time.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Data model

```
DAF Provider (you)
 └── Account     (one of your donors, linked to DAFpay)
      └── Fund        (one or many per account)
           └── Grant Request   (donation intent)
```

A **Donor** is identified by a verified phone number and is created when an individual signs up through the DAFpay payment option. You can read donors that are linked to one of your accounts, but you cannot create or modify them.

An **Account** links a donor to your organization. One donor can have accounts with multiple DAF Providers.

A **Fund** belongs to an account. You can add or update funds at any time; donors with multiple funds choose which to give from when they submit a grant.

A **Grant Request** is a donation intent created by a donor through the DAFpay payment option. You process it in your system, then report the outcome back to DAFpay.
