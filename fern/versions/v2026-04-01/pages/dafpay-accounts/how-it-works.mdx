subtitle: "From creating an account to processing a grant in four steps."

---

<Steps>

### Create an account for a donor

Create a [DAFpay account](/api/dafpay-accounts/create) for one of your donors. The account starts in `pending` status with no donor linked. DAFpay returns an `auth_code` and an `invite_url`.

<EndpointRequestSnippet endpoint="POST /v1/dafpay/accounts" />

### Share the invite with the donor

Share the `invite_url` (or just the `auth_code`) with the donor through a trusted channel — your portal, a signed email, or any other authenticated communication.

When the donor signs up on DAFpay using the invite URL or enters the auth code manually, they're automatically linked and the account moves to `approved`.

<Info>
A donor may sign up on DAFpay on their own and select your organization before you create their account. In that case the account is created in `requested` status. Acknowledge it by setting `external_account_id` via [PATCH](/api/dafpay-accounts/update) — that moves the account to `pending` and waits for the donor to verify.
</Info>

### Register the donor's funds

Once the account is created, register one or more [funds](/api/dafpay-funds/create) under it. The `name` you provide is displayed to the donor in the DAFpay payment option, so make it recognizable.

<EndpointRequestSnippet endpoint="POST /v1/dafpay/accounts/{account_id}/funds" />

### Process grant requests

When the donor gives to a nonprofit through DAFpay, you receive a `grant_request.created` webhook (or you can poll [GET /v1/dafpay/grant_requests](/api/dafpay-grant-requests/list)).

Once the account is `approved`, the grant request transitions to `awaiting_daf_submission`. Submit the grant in your system, then call [PATCH](/api/dafpay-grant-requests/update) with `status: initiated` to confirm. If you need to reject the grant, send `status: cancelled` with a `cancellation_reason`.

<EndpointRequestSnippet endpoint="PATCH /v1/dafpay/grant_requests/{id}" />

</Steps>
