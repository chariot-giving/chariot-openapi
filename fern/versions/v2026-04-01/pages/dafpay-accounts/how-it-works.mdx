<Steps>

### Create and activate accounts

**If you create the account first:** call [POST /v1/dafpay/accounts](/api/dafpay-accounts/create). The account starts in **`pending`**. DAFpay returns an `auth_code` and `invite_url` — share those with the donor through a trusted channel. When they enter the `auth_code` in DAFpay, the account becomes **`approved`**.

**If the donor used DAFpay first:** they may already have an account in **`requested`** status. The donor receives an `auth_code` to give you. Call [POST /v1/dafpay/accounts/activate](/api/dafpay-accounts/activate) with that `auth_code`.

<EndpointRequestSnippet endpoint="POST /v1/dafpay/accounts" />

<EndpointRequestSnippet endpoint="POST /v1/dafpay/accounts/activate" />

### Process grants

When the donor gives to a nonprofit through DAFpay, you receive a `grant_request.created` webhook (or you can poll [GET /v1/dafpay/grant_requests](/api/dafpay-grant-requests/list)).

Once the account is `approved`, the grant request transitions to `awaiting_daf_submission`. Submit the grant in your system, then call [PATCH /v1/dafpay/grant_requests/{id}](/api/dafpay-grant-requests/update) with `status: initiated` to confirm. If you need to reject the grant, send `status: cancelled` with a `cancellation_reason`.

<EndpointRequestSnippet endpoint="PATCH /v1/dafpay/grant_requests/{id}" />

</Steps>
