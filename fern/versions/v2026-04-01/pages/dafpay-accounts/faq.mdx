<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="**How is this different from the DAFpay payment option?**">
  The DAFpay payment option is the embeddable widget fundraisers add to their site so donors can give from their DAF. DAFpay Accounts is the API DAF Providers use to *power* that experience — onboarding donors, registering their funds, and processing the grants they submit.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="**Can I create donors directly?**">
  No. Donors are created when an individual signs up through the DAFpay payment option. As a DAF Provider, you can read donors that are linked to one of your accounts, but you cannot create or modify them.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="**Can I move an account from `pending` to `approved` myself?**">
  No. For accounts you created (`pending`), only the donor can complete verification by entering the `auth_code` in DAFpay. That transition is not available on the Update API.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="**How do I activate a `requested` account?**">
  The donor gives you the `auth_code` they received from DAFpay. Call [POST /v1/dafpay/accounts/activate](/api/dafpay-accounts/activate) with `auth_code` set to that value (and optionally `external_account_id`). Chariot finds the matching `requested` account for your organization and sets it to `approved`. You do not need the account `id`.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="**How do I remove an account I no longer want active?**">
  Accounts cannot be deleted — disable them instead. Set `status` to `disabled` via [PATCH](/api/dafpay-accounts/update). A disabled account stops accepting grant requests but preserves its history, and you (or the donor) can re-enable it at any time.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="**Can I create grant requests via the API?**">
  No. Grant requests are created exclusively by donors through the DAFpay payment option. You can list, view, and update their status (to `initiated` once you've processed them, or `cancelled` to reject them) — but you cannot create new ones.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="**When does `awaiting_account_approval` transition to `awaiting_daf_submission`?**">
  Automatically, when the parent account reaches `approved` status. You don't need to make any API call.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
