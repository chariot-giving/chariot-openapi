Chariot is the leading payments platform for charitable giving.
We help connect nonprofits and donors to revolutionize traditional offline giving, saving time and money for everyone involved.

## Documentation Structure

Our documentation is organized by audience to help you find what you need quickly:

### For Fundraisers

If you're a nonprofit or fundraising platform looking to accept donations:

- **[DAFpay](/guides/for-fundraisers/dafpay)** - Learn how to integrate DAFpay, the first ever payment option for Donor Advised Funds, to supercharge your fundraising efforts.

### For Grantmakers

If you're a grantmaker (DAF provider) looking to manage donor relationships and process payments:

- **[Disbursements](/guides/for-grantmakers/disbursements)** - Learn how Chariot Deposit Accounts<span title="Chariot is a financial technology company, not a bank. Chariot Accounts come with a Demand Deposit Account through our banking services partner, Column N.A., Member FDIC. Deposits in Chariot Accounts are eligible for FDIC insurance up to $250,000 per depositor, for each insurable capacity in which the account is held."><sup><Icon icon="fa-regular fa-circle-info" size="2.5"/></sup></span> can help streamline payments to nonprofit organizations at a fraction of the cost of sending a check.

- **[Donor Accounts](/guides/for-grantmakers/donor-accounts)** - Learn how to manage Donor Accounts and process grant requests submitted through DAFpay.
